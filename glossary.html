GYO — Gyokuro, »Jewel Dew«

Gyokuro is one of most high quality Teas Japan has to offer. Of course also within this type of Tea there are different stages of quality. This type of Tea grows for 21 days in the shade before beeing harvested. Gyokuro is grown on a special variety of the Camellia Sinensis, such as Asahi, Okumidori, Yamakai, and Saemidori. This Tea is in need of special care, when prepared. The name »Jewel Dew« refers to the pale green color of the Tea.

Tea Leaves ~ 6-10 g / 180 ml
Temperature ~ 50-60 °C
Brewing Time ~ 01:30 Minutes
Prices > 3500 ¥ [> 29,00 €]

The highest quality Gyokuros can be infused with a water temperature of ~ 40 °C.
